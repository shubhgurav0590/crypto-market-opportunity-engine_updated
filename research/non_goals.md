# Non-Goals of This Research

To avoid overclaiming and misuse, the following are explicitly NOT goals of this research.

## The system does NOT:
- Predict wars, earthquakes, pandemics, or black-swan events.
- Guarantee profits or eliminate losses.
- Claim to outperform all strategies or markets.
- Encourage leverage or high-risk speculation.
- Replace professional financial advice.
- Optimize for maximum short-term profit.

## Scope Limitations
- The system is designed primarily for BTC markets.
- Results may not generalize equally to all assets.
- Performance depends on market conditions and user behavior.

## Ethical Boundaries
- Users are never forced to trade.
- Risk limits cannot be bypassed easily.
- The system prioritizes user safety over engagement or excitement.

These non-goals ensure the research remains ethical, realistic, and responsible.
