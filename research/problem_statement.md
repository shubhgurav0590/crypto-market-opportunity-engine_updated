📄 Problem Statement (Simple & Clear Version)
1. Why this problem matters

Crypto markets move very fast and change suddenly. Prices can rise sharply, fall heavily, or stay unstable for long periods.
Most people who invest in crypto—especially those with limited savings—do not have proper tools to manage risk. They often lose money because:

They react emotionally

They trade too frequently

They enter trades late

They don’t know when to stop

Many tools and trading bots promise profits, but they focus only on buying and selling, not on protecting money. When markets crash or behave unexpectedly, these systems fail and people lose a large part of their capital.

2. What is the real problem

The real problem is not predicting prices correctly.

The real problem is:

How can we help people protect their money and make safer decisions in unpredictable crypto markets?

Most existing systems:

Assume markets behave the same all the time

Trust model predictions too much

Ignore uncertainty and risk

Do not stop trading when conditions are dangerous

This causes heavy losses, especially for people with small or moderate capital.

3. What is missing today

Current crypto trading systems usually:

Measure success using profit or accuracy only

Do not adapt when market conditions change

Do not explain why a trade is taken

Do not prevent users from trading during risky periods

Do not focus on long-term survival of capital

There is very little work focused on protecting users first, especially during unstable or extreme market conditions.

4. What this research tries to do

This research aims to build a Financial Risk Guardian System instead of a simple trading bot.

The system will:

Understand different market conditions (stable, volatile, risky)

Measure how confident or uncertain the system is

Detect early signs of market stress

Reduce or stop trading when risk is high

Use small, controlled risk instead of aggressive trades

Help users avoid emotional and harmful decisions

The goal is not maximum profit, but safe and sustainable participation in crypto markets.

5. What this research does NOT claim

This system:

Does not predict wars, earthquakes, or disasters

Does not guarantee profits

Does not remove all losses

Does not replace human judgment

Instead, it focuses on reducing damage when markets behave badly.

6. Why this is important for common people

Many people invest money they cannot afford to lose.
A single bad decision can wipe out months or years of savings.

This research is designed to:

Protect small investors

Reduce unnecessary losses

Encourage patience and discipline

Make crypto participation safer and more understandable

7. What makes this different

This work is different because it:

Prioritizes capital protection over profit

Treats “not trading” as a valid decision

Measures success by how well money survives

Designs the system around real human behavior

In one sentence (very important)

This research focuses on building a system that helps people survive crypto markets safely, not chase risky profits.
